Detailed Description

The Item web service contains methods for querying and retrieving information about items. PATHS items are information derived from Europeana and Alinari and includes most attributes defined by the Europeana Semantic Elements. Items have been enriched with (1) background links, (2) topic links and (3) item similarity links.
